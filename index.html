Play Asteroids

Instructions

HOW TO PLAY:

Movement: WASD keys to move, 'Enter' key to shoot, 'R' to warp.

Warping: You will be transported to a random location and be invulnerable for a short period of time.

Respawn: When you respawn you will be invulnerable for a short period of time.

Invulnerability: You have some periods of grace, during this time your ship will appear green.

Objective: Destroy all the asteroids to advance to the next level.

Be Careful: You only have 3 lives! Asteroids bounce off each other.

Scoring: Score will be determined by how many asteroids you destroy.

GOOD LUCK!
